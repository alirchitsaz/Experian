1. Core Value Proposition (The Elevator Pitch)

The Hook: "I sit at the intersection of complex technology and human behavior."

The Pitch: Experian needs a leader who can take ChatGPT Enterprise and scale it globally without creating security nightmares or fragmented toolsets. My expertise lies in architecting governed, scalable platforms within Global ITSM and Digital Workplace environments, while serving as a key voice on AI Center of Excellence boards. I don't just deploy tech; I find the 'carrot' that drives user adoption, building custom workflows and agents that create immediate competitive advantages while strictly adhering to compliance, legal, and risk frameworks.

4. STAR Story Bank (Fill in your specifics)

Pro Tip: When answering, keep the "Situation" and "Task" brief (20%). Spend 60% of your time on the "Action" (what YOU did), and 20% on the quantifiable "Result".

Story 1: The Governance & CoE Play (Safe Operations)

Use when asked about: Risk, Legal, Compliance, avoiding fragmented AI.

S Situation: The business was rapidly exploring GenAI, creating a risk of fragmented tools, "Shadow AI," and potential data vulnerabilities.
T Task: Needed to establish a unified governance framework without suffocating innovation.
A Action: Through involvement with the AI Center of Excellence, partnered with InfoSec, Legal, and Privacy. Defined access standards, regulated data controls, and created a structured intake/evaluation process for new AI capabilities.
R Result: [Insert Metric] Achieved controlled scaling, mitigated compliance risks, and provided a single point of accountability for leadership.

Story 2: The ITSM / Digital Workplace Rollout (Scale)

Use when asked about: Global platforms, Service Management integration, operational support.

S Situation: Needed to deploy a major digital workplace platform across a global, multi-regional organization.
T Task: Ensure seamless integration with existing Identity and Service Management structures while maintaining stable operations post-rollout.
A Action: Leveraged Global ITSM frameworks. Coordinated the vendor/SI handoff, defined the long-term operating model, and managed the release cycle in a phased approach.
R Result: [Insert Metric] Smooth enterprise-wide adoption with zero disruption to existing business lines.

Story 3: Building Custom GPTs & "Finding the Carrot" (Adoption)

Use when asked about: Hands-on agent building, multi-persona use cases, driving adoption.

S Situation: Teams had access to AI tools but adoption was low, or workflows were highly manual and inefficient.
T Task: Deliver a targeted AI solution that users would actually want to adopt.
A Action: Applied hands-on workflow automation expertise to build custom GPTs/Agents tailored to specific personas. Focused heavily on the human element—identifying the "carrot" that made their daily jobs easier rather than just pushing a mandate.
R Result: [Insert Metric] High user adoption, measured productivity outcomes, and clear value realization reported to the C-suite.
