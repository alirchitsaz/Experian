The Intersection of Tech & Humans

A successful ChatGPT Enterprise rollout isn't just an IT deployment. It’s a behavioral shift.

1. Finding the "Carrot"

Tools fail when they feel like mandates. My expertise is identifying multi-persona use cases and building custom GPTs/Agents that solve real daily friction. I don't push technology; I deliver the carrot that organically drives adoption and productivity.

2. Scale Without Vulnerability

Drawing on deep experience directing Global ITSM and Digital Workplace initiatives, I understand how to integrate new tools with existing Identity, Security, and Service Management architectures. I build systems that scale globally without breaking.

3. Defending the Governance

Serving on AI Center of Excellence boards has taught me how to partner effectively with Legal, Privacy, and Risk. I establish the guardrails first, ensuring responsible data controls and audit readiness without suffocating business innovation.

The 90-Day Execution Roadmap

Addressing the core requirements of the Head of Global Workplace GenAI role.

Days 1-30: Governance & Alignment

Focus: Establish single-point ownership and stop fragmented accountability.

  • Partner with Experian InfoSec, Legal, and Privacy to lock in data controls for ChatGPT Enterprise.
  • Map out the vendor/SI handoff and establish the long-term ITSM operating model.
  • Audit existing "Shadow AI" toolsets across the organization.

Days 31-60: The Controlled Rollout

Focus: Phased adoption and multi-persona use cases.

  • Coordinate with Digital Workplace & Identity teams for seamless SSO and access provisioning.
  • Develop targeted Custom GPTs for high-impact departments (e.g., HR, Sales, IT Support) to demonstrate immediate value.
  • Define success metrics (daily active users, task time reduction).

Days 61-90: Value Realization & Scale

Focus: Reporting and establishing the release cycle.

  • Report platform performance and tangible ROI to senior leadership (SVP of Digital Workplace).
  • Establish the intake and evaluation process for *new* OpenAI capabilities as they are released.
  • Expand rollout to international regions under unified governance standards.

Proven Execution (STAR Methodology)

How my background maps directly to Experian's strategic needs.

Situation: The business was rapidly exploring GenAI, creating a risk of fragmented tools, "Shadow AI," and potential data vulnerabilities.
Task: Establish a unified governance framework and secure operating model without acting as a bottleneck to innovation.
Action: Through leadership on an AI Center of Excellence board, I partnered cross-functionally with Legal, Privacy, and Risk. I helped define strict access standards, regulated data controls, and created a structured intake/evaluation process for reviewing and safely rolling out new AI capabilities.
Result: Mitigated compliance risks, established a single point of accountability for AI tooling, and created a safe "sandbox" for the enterprise to innovate securely.
Situation: Required to deploy major digital workplace platforms across a global, multi-regional organization while avoiding disruption.
Task: Ensure seamless integration with existing Identity and Service Management structures and take ownership of the post-implementation operating model.
Action: Leveraged deep expertise in Global ITSM. Coordinated the vendor and SI handoff processes, embedded the new technology into the standard service catalog, and managed the release cycle in a highly controlled, phased approach across global offices.
Result: Achieved stable enterprise-wide adoption, seamless coordination with underlying identity teams, and robust ongoing operational support.
Situation: Teams had access to basic automation and AI, but adoption was low because the tools lacked specific context for their daily workflows.
Task: Deliver targeted AI solutions (like Custom GPTs or Agents) that users would genuinely want to use—finding the "carrot."
Action: Applied hands-on workflow automation expertise (drawn from experience architecting specialized AI solutions) to build custom, persona-based agents. Focused heavily on change management and user experience, mapping the AI capabilities directly to painful, manual bottlenecks.
Result: Drove high organic adoption rates, tracked clear productivity outcomes, and successfully reported measurable value realization back to senior leadership.
