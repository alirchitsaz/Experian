The Problem I Solve

The Gap Between
Having AI &
Using AI.

Every enterprise buys the platform. Almost none unlock the value. The failure mode is always the same: fragmented ownership, ungoverned rollouts, and employees who are anxious instead of empowered.

01

Fragmented Accountability

Multiple teams deploy AI tools with no single owner. Security gaps form. Duplication wastes budget. No one reports to leadership on real value.

02

The Fear Factor

When leadership introduces ChatGPT Enterprise, the first question isn't "How do I use it?" — it's "Will I be replaced?" Anxiety kills adoption.

03

Governance Debt

Legal, Privacy, and InfoSec aren't at the table early enough. Regulated-data controls are bolted on after the fact instead of built in from day one.
